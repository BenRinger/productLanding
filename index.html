Scuba Unlimited

Come on in the waters fine

In 1974, Scuba Unlimited opened its doors in Cincinnati giving area residents the opportunity to experience the underwater world from a close up view. We are a full service scuba and snorkeling center focusing on our commitment to our customers and our dedication to education. We are also the exclusive dive center for the Newport Aquarium. Our knowledgeable non-commission staff can help you find the right equipment to fit your personal needs for rental or purchase and continue our well known customer service with maintenance and repair. For a partial list of the products we carry, please look at our products page. Education is the cornerstone of the Scuba Unlimited philosophy having certified thousands of divers locally and around the world. We currently have 30 instructors and divemasters, with a wide variety of backgrounds and occupations, giving us a low student to teacher ratio and a diverse perspective. We educate students on the basics to the technical aspects of scuba, as well as the importance of safety and the preservation of the environment. Our dedication has also expanded outside of our store to the community. In conjunction with the Cincinnati Recreation Commission, Scuba Unlimited has developed a Discover Scuba program to give our inner city children an opportunity to experience the underwater world. Weekly Discover Scuba sessions are held at area city pools during the summer, followed by an Open Water class and a certification trip to the Florida Springs.

Scuba Unlimited is committed to making Scuba education and training as safe, fun and flexible as possible. Please see our training calendar for a class that is convenient for your schedule. If you prefer a private class please contact us so we can discuss your time requirements. If you are already a diver and have not been in the water for a while, please call us about our Scuba Tune Up Offer. Earning your “Certification Card (C-Card)” is a three step process: First: you participate in classroom and home study sessions. Second: You will learn and practice essential scuba diving lessons in the pool. Third: You will apply your new skills and knowledge by making training dives in a lake or ocean. After that….. Beyond the Open Water Certification, Scuba Unlimited offers a number of advanced or specialty classes for both recreational and professional divers. We feel that we are fortunate to have the best instructor’s and divemasters teaching our classes and leading our trips. We look forward to diving with you.
